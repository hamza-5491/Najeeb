Features

The platform provides trained teachers on specific teaching courses for each teacher, thus ensuring the quality of performance and teaching by the teacher
Lessons are recorded audio and video so that the student can Review it again, and the guardian can follow his son
The platform provides male teachers to teach boys
The platform is provided to teachers around the clock, allowing the student to start the class at any time and immediately
The platform provides women teachers to teach girls
Class prices are calculated by the minute, which gives flexibility in time for the student to consume what he needs
Teachers' prices for the specified course are the same, so the student does not need to compare prices
The lesson is delivered from the teacher to the student using direct video viewing, as well as by sharing the screen to explain the materials, as well as using an interactive smart board that allows the teacher and student to write on one board
